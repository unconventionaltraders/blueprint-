Phase Flow
Three-Phase Execution
01
HTF Liquidity Sweep + Market Shift
Identify a higher timeframe liquidity level (swing high/low, BSL/SSL) that has been swept. A sweep alone is not enough — wait for a confirmed market structure shift (MSS) on the same or next lower timeframe to validate the sweep as genuine reversal intent, not just a wick hunt.

Valid signal: Price sweeps the level AND closes back through it with displacement, breaking a prior swing in the opposite direction.
4H · 15M BIAS FRAME
02
IFVG Entry on 1M
Drop to the 1-minute chart and locate a bearish FVG. Wait for price to close above it — this invalidates the bearish gap and converts it into an Inverted FVG (IFVG) long setup. Enter long on the retest of that IFVG zone.

The IFVG must be directionally aligned with the HTF bias confirmed in Phase 01. Do not take counter-trend IFVGs.
1M ENTRY FRAME
03
Confluence Confirmation
Before pulling the trigger, both FTWM and Volumetric Spectrogram conditions must be met. See sections below. If either fails — no trade.

Indicator · Gate 02
Volumetric Spectrogram Conditions
Upper Oscillator · Dotted Line
Volume Above Price (Resistance)
Must be expanding — increasing activity above price signals that distribution/overhead pressure is being absorbed or building participation. Look for the dotted line rising or starting to pulse upward as price enters the IFVG zone.
Lower Oscillator · Solid Line
Volume Below Price (Support)
Must be becoming less negative, trending toward zero. A solid line moving from deep negative toward zero confirms the support floor is strengthening. It does not need to go positive — the directional shift is the signal. Colour is optional and secondary.
📊
Reading the combination: Upper expanding + lower less-negative = volume is activating on both sides. This is the participation signature of a genuine move, not a noise wick. Background colour (green/red) on 3M for bias, 1M for timing — but colour alone is never sufficient.

Entry Checklist

Dotted line (vol above price) is rising or pulsing upward at the time of entry.
✅
Lower Oscillator Rising
